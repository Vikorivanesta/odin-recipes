Recipe For Betrayal

Ingredients

  • One generous helping of envy and jealousy
  • A hefty portion of greed and selfishness
  • One cup of resentment and anger
  • One ounce of fear and paranoia
  • A pinch of manipulation and deceit
  • A sprinkle of dishonesty and backstabbing
  • One teaspoon of abandonment and betrayal

Steps

  1. Find a person you envy or hate, and focus your envious and jealous feelings towards them.
  2. Let your greed and selfishness consume you, making you only want more and more.
  3. Gather resentment and anger, and let them eat away at your sense of morals and empathy.
  4. Let fear and paranoia slowly consume your reason, until you become paranoid and delusional.
  5. Learn and practice manipulation and deceit, so you can fool the people around you.
  6. Use your dishonesty and backstabbing to manipulate and backstab the person you envy or hate, hurting them in the process and fulfilling your selfish and envious needs.
