Recipe For Disaster

Ingredients

  • Three tablespoons of prejudice
  • Three spoonfuls of ignorance
  • Two teaspoons of ego
  • Two ounces of narcissism
  • One cup of hatred
  • One tablespoon of revenge

Steps

  1. Mix together equal parts prejudice and ignorance
  2. Add a generous helping of ego and narcissism
  3. Sprinkle some hatred and revenge overtop
  4. Simmer over a long period of time, until the mixture boils over and causes an almighty, monumental explosion of chaos and destruction.
