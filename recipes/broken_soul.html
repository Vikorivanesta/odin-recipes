Recipe For Broken Soul

Ingredients

  • A generous helping of guilt and self-loathing
  • A hefty portion of pain and suffering
  • A dash of loneliness and isolation
  • One cup of depression and hopelessness
  • One ounce of despair and sorrow
  • One teaspoon of trauma and abuse
  • A sprinkle of abandonment and betrayal

Steps

  1. Mix together the ingredients in a bowl and stir well.
  2. Let the ingredients ferment and marinate for a long time, until they become dark and bitter.
  3. Once the mixture is fully fermented, you will have a fully developed mixture of broken soul.
